My hobbies

I don't have a lot of hobbies as I am not an active person, but I can name a few:

Perhaps a common thing for a person to be interested in drawing. I didn't do it in a while, probably should start again. Drawing takes a lot of practice and also a lot of patience to learn it; much like programming. That's why I admire people who can draw well.
Kendo (1 kyu)
Japanese fencing so to say. Started training on summer 2018.
Programming
Can't say I am a proffesionalist. Because what professionalist is 17 y.o.?.. Anyway, this is my main activity and IT is the sphere I'd like to continue working in.
Gaming
My first relationships with PCs started in age of 10-11; and maybe as for a lot of people, they started with games. I like gaming and have quite a lot of good moments of my life related to it. Gaming was the main driving force for my interest towards programming. If not for games, maybe I'd never get to learn more about PCs. In fact, the PC when I was a kid, is the same PC I have now; or is it better to say, upgraded version of it.
Music
Listening to music as a hobby has become very common cliche thing nowadays; to the extent where teens at my age don't even consider it a hobby. But it's not the fact that you are listening to it, what makes it special; it's what you are listening to, what really matters. The mix of genres and artists, whom you listen to, are always different; they form your exclusive listening style. This is a lot like how people listen to each other, not everyone makes up a good listener.
